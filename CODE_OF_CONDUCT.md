# Code of Conduct

FathomSeed is a learning project. The community should feel useful, thoughtful, and safe to participate in.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, but fix real problems.
- Explain your reasoning when you disagree.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect different learning styles and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Publicly exposing private information
- Spam
- Repeatedly derailing discussions
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid work

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who make the project harder for others to join.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainers through the repository.

# 行为准则

FathomSeed 是一个学习产品项目。社区应该让人觉得有用、清醒、并且愿意参与。

## 期待的行为

- 友善，也直接。
- 默认善意，但认真修正真实问题。
- 不同意时说明理由。
- 反馈尽量具体、可行动。
- 尊重不同学习方式和背景。

## 不接受的行为

- 骚扰或人身攻击
- 歧视
- 公开他人隐私信息
- 垃圾信息
- 反复带偏讨论
- 强迫维护者做无偿劳动

## 处理方式

维护者可以删除评论、关闭 issue，或屏蔽让他人难以参与项目的人。

如果你看到问题，可以通过仓库联系维护者。
